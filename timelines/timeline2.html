Parent Outreach Workers

The POW team continues to provide support to local families in need, offering emotional support as families juggle new challenges of home-schooling and even greater isolation. This team is also supporting families to access food, basic need, and activity kits to engage in with their families. The team has nearly 75 interactions with families each week via 1:1 support

We Breastfeed & Chestfeed

We Breastfeed & Chestfeed provides family-friendly social space and infant feeding support for pregnant people and parents in Guelph. Amidst the pandemic, individual and group-based support is taking place virtually, by phone and video connections, with Trained Peer-Parents providing care for 15 parents and their children each week
